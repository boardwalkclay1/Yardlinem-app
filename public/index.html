Common Questions

How do you price yard work?

We base pricing on yard size, service type, and current condition. You’ll always see a clear quote before you approve.

What if it rains on my scheduled day?

If weather makes it unsafe or ineffective to work, we’ll reschedule you as soon as possible and notify you in the app.

Do I need to be home during service?

No, as long as we have access to the yard and any gates or codes we need.
