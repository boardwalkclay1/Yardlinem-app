Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about our yard care services.

How do you price yard work?

We base pricing on yard size, service type, and current condition. You’ll always see a clear quote before you approve.

What areas do you serve?

We currently serve neighborhoods within our local region. Contact us with your address and we’ll confirm.

What if it rains on my scheduled day?

If weather makes it unsafe or ineffective to work, we’ll reschedule you as soon as possible and notify you.

Do I need to be home during service?

No, as long as we have access to the yard and any gates or codes we need.

How do I reschedule or cancel?

You can contact us directly or, once the app is live, manage your schedule from your phone.
