Services

Everything we can do to keep your yard clean, safe, and looking sharp.

Lawn Mowing

We provide consistent mowing for small to medium‑sized residential yards, with attention to detail around edges and obstacles.

  • Standard cut height based on season and grass type
  • Blowing clippings off hard surfaces
  • Optional bagging available

Best for: weekly or bi‑weekly maintenance.

Edging & Trimming

We sharpen the lines along sidewalks, driveways, and beds, and trim around fences, trees, and obstacles.

Can be added to mowing or booked as a standalone service.

Leaf & Debris Cleanup

Seasonal cleanups to remove leaves, branches, and yard debris from your lawn and beds.

  • Raking and blowing leaves into piles
  • Bagging or hauling away debris (depending on your selection)

Bush & Hedge Trimming

We shape and maintain bushes, hedges, and small shrubs to keep them healthy and visually balanced.

Mulch & Bed Refresh

We remove weeds, clean up beds, and lay fresh mulch to restore color and contrast.

Haul‑Away & Debris Removal

We can haul away yard waste and debris so you’re not left with a pile at the curb.
